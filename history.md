# The History of the Glossa

To understand the [[welcome]] document, one must understand the history of commentary.

## Medieval Origins

In the 12th century, the *Glossa Ordinaria* became the standard biblical commentary. It was not a separate book, but lived **in the margins** of the text itself.

> "The text provides the structure, but the commentary provides the life."

This tradition of interlinked thought is what we aim to revive. Unlike a modern hyperlink which whisks you away, a wiki-link in this context suggests a related room in the same library.
